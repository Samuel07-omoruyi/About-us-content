About Porsche

Our Heritage

Founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche, Porsche AG has established itself as a global leader in high-performance automobiles. With roots in German engineering excellence, Porsche continues to push the boundaries of innovation, combining power, performance, and precision in every vehicle. Our story began with the iconic Porsche 356, and since then, we have set new standards in the sports car industry.

Innovation and Performance

At Porsche, we believe that every car we create should embody a unique blend of tradition and innovation. From our groundbreaking engineering in aerodynamics and electric vehicle technology to our legendary six-cylinder boxer engines, Porsche's commitment to precision and performance is unrivaled. Whether you are driving a classic 911 or the all-electric Taycan, you are experiencing over 70 years of passion and pursuit of perfection.

Luxury Meets Technology

Each Porsche is designed to provide more than just speed. It offers an unparalleled driving experience. With luxurious interiors, cutting-edge technology, and superior craftsmanship, Porsche vehicles are designed for those who demand the best. Our commitment to innovation ensures that every Porsche is equipped with advanced driver assistance systems, infotainment technologies, and sustainable engineering to shape the future of driving.

Our Mission

At Porsche, our mission is simple: to create sports cars that excite the imagination, inspire passion, and deliver a truly unforgettable driving experience. We believe that every drive should be an adventure, and every vehicle we produce should reflect the highest standards of craftsmanship, design, and performance.
